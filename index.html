Who We Are

A Foundation Built on the Belief That Everyone Deserves Better

InAmigos Foundation is a registered non-governmental organisation working across India since 2020. Born from a collective of passionate changemakers, we address some of the most pressing social challenges — from child education and food insecurity to animal welfare and environmental crisis.

Our name means "Friends Together." That spirit guides everything we do — building bridges between those who have and those who need, creating communities of care, and turning goodwill into measurable impact.

Mission

To empower underserved communities through inclusive, sustainable programs that restore dignity and create opportunity.

Vision

An India where every child, woman, animal, and environment is protected, nurtured, and allowed to thrive.

Our Work

Six Initiatives, One Purpose

Each initiative addresses a specific dimension of social need. Together, they form a complete ecosystem of change.

📚
Education

Bachpanshala

Providing quality education, learning materials, and mentorship to children from underserved communities — because every childhood deserves a classroom.

Shaping futures through literacy and learning
🍱
Food & Clothing

Seva

Running food drives, clothing distributions, and essential supplies support for families facing poverty and displacement across India.

Ensuring no one goes hungry or cold
🐾
Animal Welfare

Jeev

Rescuing, rehabilitating, and caring for stray and injured animals — because compassion has no species boundary.

Protecting lives beyond the human world
🌸
Women Empowerment

Udaan

Supporting women through education, vocational training, legal awareness, and community leadership programs that break cycles of dependency.

Lifting women so communities rise with them
🌿
Environment

Prakriti

Tree plantation drives, waste management campaigns, and ecological education to protect the natural world for future generations.

Healing the planet, one tree at a time
🔧
Skill Development

Vikas

Equipping youth and adults with vocational skills, digital literacy, and entrepreneurship training to access better livelihoods.

Building skills that open doors to opportunity
