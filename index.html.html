Who We Are

About InAmigos Foundation

InAmigos Foundation is a registered Section 8 NGO in India committed to practical ground reforms. Under our theme "Uniting Minds for Change", we establish direct partnerships with community networks, schools, and stray rescue hubs.

Our Core Principle

"We don't wait for policy. We implement positive changes together today."

🎯

Our Mission

InAmigos Pillar of Action

To empower and safeguard vulnerable human populations and stray animals by bringing passionate minds together. Through direct community distribution, localized educational classes, veterinary aid, and eco-initiatives, we establish robust support frameworks representing compassion and transparency.

  • ✓ 100% Direct resource routing for high integrity
  • ✓ Empirical child-led study centers (InAmigos Shiksha)
  • ✓ Daily street animal nourishment networks (Project Jeev)

📖 Education
15K+ Children Educated

Education Support Initiatives

Providing bridge learning, school supplies, and computer literacy to underprivileged classrooms.

Under our 'InAmigos Shiksha' track, we serve disadvantaged neighborhoods by establishing custom community study centers, distributing modern learning materials, and delivering hands-on computer workshops.

🌿 Environment
50K+ Saplings Planted

Environmental Awareness Campaigns

Leading mass tree plantation, waste management updates, and local water sanitization drives.

Our Earth First collective organizes weekly tree plantation sessions ('GoGreen'), executes cleanup drives at critical freshwater points, and creates citizen workshops on circular recycling.

🐾 Animal Welfare
8K+ Strays Nourished

Project Jeev – Animal Welfare

Feeding thousands of stray animals daily, administering basic first-aid, and managing adoptions.

Project Jeev is our dedicated companion animal feeding and rescue chain. Our teams set up water bowls, provide wholesome daily food, and coordinate veterinary adoptions.

👥 Community
20K+ Families Assisted

Community Development Programs

Distributing hygiene toolkits, conducting wellness camps, and equipping low-income clusters.

Our neighborhood recovery scheme supports families with pure drinking water canisters, sanitation kits, and free health camps by registered general physicians.

⚡ Youth Empowerment
10K+ Careers Guided

Youth Empowerment Activities

Mentoring young minds with digital training, drafting workshops, and resume coaching sessions.

We host digital workspace seminars equipping students with basic computing, CV construction classes, and professional communication hacks.
